Event Information

  • 27 Feb 2018
  • 20:00
  • Free entrance

Description:

The brand new Don’t Touch My Mushroom show has arrived! Returning to the friendly location of jenseits im Viadukt we will improvise unique stories to make you laugh, cry and think!

Drinks are sold at the bar before the show and during the break. You are also very welcome to stay for a drink and a chat with the talented Mushrooms - but remember: Don’t Touch!

The show is in English. Please register also through the jenseits event page.

Looking forward to seeing you on February 27th - the fun starts at 20:00!
