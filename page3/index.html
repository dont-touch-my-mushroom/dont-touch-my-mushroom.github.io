Don't Touch My Mushroom is

Improv troupe

We are an amateur improv troupe who love playing improv!

International

Our members come from all over the world and all have their own special accent.

Giving shows

We do our own shows but we also participate in "competitive" improv.

Zurich based

We rehearse and perform in Zurich (Switzerland).

Fun

We love doing improv and sharing it with our audience.

Donation based

We don't charge for tickets for our shows.

The Harold

The Harold is a longform improvised format which emphasises patterns, themes and group discoveries instead of a traditional plot or story. Everything starts from a simple suggestion from the audience.
