Don't Touch My Mushroom

  • Stay informed on English Improv events in Zurich!
  • For improvisers, join our open sessions on Thursdays.
  • Follow us on Facebook if you like.
  • Something else? Get in touch.

We are organizing roughly biweekly open sessions – everyone with some Improv experience is welcome.

How to join?

  • Join our mailing list improv-zurich.
  • Introduce yourself informally. A “Hi” is fine, but you can go fancy ;)
  • Show up.

Where is it?

See this document for an illustrated guide.

How much does it cost?

Payment is absolutely optional. That said, we appreciate donations towards the room rent from everyone who can afford it and enjoys the session – e.g. CHF 10.

We might change this if this doesn’t work out but so far it is looking good.
