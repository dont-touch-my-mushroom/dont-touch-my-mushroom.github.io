Premiere at Jenseits!

07 Nov 2017

Dear friends & improv friends,

The Improv Group Don’t Touch My Mushroom is proud… and slightly scared… to announce its first independent show in English!

In a friendly atmosphere of Viadukt, we will perform some well-known and some less so well-known improv games and try to make your face shine with a smile throughout one dark November evening.

Drinks will be sold at a bar. Some small snacks will be provided on spot too.

Looking forward to seeing you on November 7th - the fun starts at 20:00!
