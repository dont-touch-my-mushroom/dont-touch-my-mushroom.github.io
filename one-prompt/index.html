One Prompt is a YouTube video project of “Don’t Touch My Mushroom”: One prompt to inspire them all. The same suggestions are given to multiple teams so that they can show off their individual style and interpretation.

Subscribe not to miss any videos!

If you are interested in participating, let us know via this form or via our contact form.
